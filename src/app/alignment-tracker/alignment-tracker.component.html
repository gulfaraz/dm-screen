Input Character Name


Enter the character name then click "Add Character". This will add a coloured marker to the graph to track the characters alignment.



Adjust Alignment


Adjust the character alignment by dragging their character marker on the graph.


The axes are labelled with ethics (chaotic/lawful) along the x-axis and moral (good/evil) along the y-axis.
